Ekā

A behavioral mirror for your digital presence.

The Origin

Inspired by a child's simple question—"Do you know how many times you check your phone?"—Ekā was built to turn raw device data into a moment of mindful reflection.

The Mirror

Unlike standard reports that focus on minutes and limits, Ekā interprets the quality of your attention. By synthesizing interaction patterns into states of Presence, State, and Equilibrium, it helps you reclaim intentionality in a world of digital noise.
