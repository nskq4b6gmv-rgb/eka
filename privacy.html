Ekā Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 Feb 2026

Ekā is built as a tool for reflection. Your attention is personal, and we treat it that way.

No Data Collection

Ekā does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We do not have access to your interaction history, usage patterns, or behavioral insights.

On-Device Processing

All data used by Ekā is processed entirely on your device using Apple’s Screen Time framework. Your information never leaves your device.

No Accounts

Ekā does not require account creation, login, or cloud synchronization.

No Tracking

Ekā does not use analytics tools, third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect data, we do not sell, share, or transfer personal information to any third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Ekā does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the app evolves in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated here.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, you may contact us via AppStore
